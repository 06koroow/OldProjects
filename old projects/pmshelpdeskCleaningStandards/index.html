Cleaning Standards

Starting with the cleaning process, you need to make sure the home is prepared, aired, obstacle free and rubbish cleared. If more than one cleaner is attending the premise, then each person will be responsible for different tasks. To allow the cleaning flow to be smooth, accurate and quick, always follow these steps:

  • COVID 19 Adjustments – follow all UK government guidelines
  • Face covering (face mask)
  • Disposable gloves
  • Hand sanitizer
  • Shoes protector
  • Use and dispose of clothes and sponges.
  • Check Veeve instructions about the property, alarm system, products and materials.
  • Ensure you are at the right property and identify the right door.
  • Ensure you have the right key and sanitise them.
  • Open all windows, but make sure that the security of the premise remains always a priority.
  • Remove all rubbish and place it next to the front door
  • Remove or put back all the obstacles impeding your passage.
  • Replace the rubbish bin bags.
  • Identify any specific surfaces needing special care.
  • Identify stains or heavy soil requiring special treatment (i.e. upholstery and carpet) and act on them first.
  • If applicable : strip all beds and place dirty linen and towels inside pillowcases ready for collection (place them at the entrance of the house unless differently specified). Linen and towels should not be shaken in case viruses are dispersed through the air. Ensure it is removed from the property prior to cleaning.
  • Remove all rugs from the floor.
  • Prepare you basket/bag with all you need for your cleaning.
  • Load the dishwasher and start the washing program.
  • Spray oven products and let it stay.
  • If any problem make sure to promptly inform Veeve, as soon as possible.

Start by dusting the whole room top to bottom, including headboard and bed frame, proceed by making the bed. Ensure mirrors and windows are smear free. Check under and behind bed and furniture where possible. Make sure to fluff pillows and cushion, to place the throw as in appendix 3 and all decorations and frames tidy and in line. Sanitise all surfaces.

  • Dust/clean headboard and bed frame
  • Make the bed with new linen.
  • Fold and place clean towels ( see appendix D)
  • Dust all surfaces and furniture using a dust stick/duster and going from the top to the bottom of the room.
  • Dust window sills and blinds/curtains-remove cob webs
  • Dust doors and door frame and skirting
  • Dust tv and frames
  • Clean windows (internally) and mirrors.
  • Sanitise all hard surfaces including doors and doors’ handle, remote controls, switches, knobs, intercom, hairdryer, windows handles.

Dust, spray, spread, stay, scrub, wipe, sanitise: after dusting, spray the products and let it sit in order to work at its best before scrubbing and wipe it. Make sure not to use abrasive scourer, bleach or bleach based chemicals or any acidic products (including vinegar). Ensure the bathroom is scale free and odor free.

Place bath mat, toilet papers and toiletries as shown in appendix D.

  • Dust any dry surface and cupboard, doors and door frame going from the top to the bottom
  • Clean and sanitise the WC (inside and outside)
  • Clean and sanitise the toilet brush
  • Clear the shower and sink drain/trap/ remove hairs
  • Clean and sanitise shower/tub, including shower head if reachable and shower glass from the inside
  • Clean and sanitise sink, inside and out
  • Clean outside of the shower door and mirror (smear free)
  • Dust the window sill and blinds
  • Clean the windows (internally)
  • Wipe tiles and skirting and walls, if any particular stain
  • Clean and sanitise sink
  • Wipe and polish fixture, toilet holder, hand rail, towels rack etc..
  • If the floor is too wet, wipe wash it.
  • Sanitise all hard surfaces including doors and doors’ handle, remote controls, switches, knobs, hairdryer, windows handles.

Start by dusting, make sure to use the right products per each surface. Dust top to bottom. Fluffy the cushions and tidy the room, organize the chairs. The space should look presentable and neat and clutter free. Sanitise all surfaces.

  • Dust all surfaces , including frames
  • Dust the skirting
  • Polish furniture
  • Dust and clean tables and chairs (above and below)
  • Dust window sill and blinds/curtains
  • Clean the windows (internally)
  • Dust lights fixture
  • Dust shelves and cupboards
  • Dust and clean cushions, sofa and upholstery (eliminate breadcrumbs, dust and residues)
  • Dust Tv and electronic devices (stereo, dvd, decoder etc..)including remotes and light switches.
  • Make up sofa bed-guest ready (if applicable)
  • Sanitise all hard surfaces including doors and doors’ handle, remote controls, switches, knobs, hairdryer, windows handles.

The outdoor space needs to look tidy and leaves free, chairs and tables need to be organized and properly placed under the table. If any clutter or cleaning equipment is there, please make sure to out in on a side. Empty any ashtray or rubbish, if any. Ensure the outdoor space looks pleasant and ready to be used.

  • Remove leaves, rubbish and empty ashtrays
  • Remove cob webs from the external windows
  • Wipe chairs and tables
  • Dust and wipe front door/doors
  • Tidy up garden, patio and entrance area.

The oven and the kettle should have already been sprayed and ready to be wiped and cleaned. Start with the dust, proceed with the oven, hobs, appliances and white goods. Leave the sink at the end in case you need to use it. Make sure the kettle is left empty. Ensure all the appliances are switched off.

  • Degreases, clean and sanitise.
  • Dust and clean any dry surface (shelves, tv, doors, skirting, doors, door frame, window sills, window and french door, etc..)
  • Wash the dishes (if not dishwasher)
  • Clean all cupboard inside and outside (including the baseboard)
  • Clean up the kettle and rinse it properly
  • Remove crumbs from toaster
  • Clean the oven and the oven rack
  • Throw away any perishable and keep the rest, tidy and neat.*
  • Ensure pots, pans, kitchenware and cutlery are clean
  • Clean and wipe the fridge inside, out, side and on top, including rubber
  • Throw away anything from the fridge and keep only what is host food (usually non-perishable left in a specific section of fridge, make space for the coming guest)
  • Clean and wipe the washing machine (inside and outside, including the drawers)
  • Clean and wipe the dishwasher, inside, out, side and top (after cycle)
  • Clean and wipe any other white goods present and the rubbish bin
  • Clear the sink trap/drain
  • Sanitise counters, sink and knobs
  • Wipe the appliances and handles
  • Wipe under and inside the appliances (i.e. under microwave)
  • Sanitise all hard surfaces including doors and doors’ handle, remote controls, switches, knobs, hairdryer, windows handles.

  • Take care of the floor, by hoovering and moping from top to bottom and from right to left. Make sure you check that all is as it should be to ensure a high level of cleanliness and presentation of the home. Look for details.
  • Hoover rugs and floor, from top to bottom and under beds, sofas and furniture, if possible.
  • Mop the floor, from top to bottom.
  • Wait for the home to be dry and place the rugs back to their original place.
  • Make sure to remove or repose all equipment. Do not leave anything behind you
  • Perform a final check for hairs or other
  • Check that gas, water and electrics are off and the house is safe
  • Secure windows and doors
  • Set the alarm, if indicated in your instructions
  • Return key to Keynest.
  • Please, don’t come back to an already cleaned property , if so wear gloves and change them before touching surfaces and door lock and keys.
