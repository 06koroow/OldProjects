WiFi Troubleshooting

A:

  • Check the House Manual or search for the router. More often than not routers are in communal rooms or hallways
  • Once located, check that the router is plugged into the power and switched on
  • Check the WiFi details on the router match those provided on the HM, if they do not try the other details and inform Ops as soon as possible.
  • If the router is on, check the status lights on the router itself. If there is an error showing (if the network isn't showing up on your device this will usually be a red light on the WiFi indicator)
  • Check that the ethernet cable is connected properly to both the router and the wall socket.
  • Perform a reset by either, switching the router off, removing the power cable, and reconnecting it after about 45 seconds. If there is a 'reset' button on the router, press and hold this untill the light on the router flash off then on again
  • Should none of the above work, please contact Ops. They will make contact with the AM's, PM's and potentially the host to help resolve the issue.
